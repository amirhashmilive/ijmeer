Become a Peer Reviewer

IJMEER relies on expert reviewers from diverse disciplines to maintain our high standards of publication quality. By joining our reviewer network, you contribute to advancing research while developing your own academic profile. See our full application page for details.

Professional Recognition
Receive certificates and formal acknowledgment of your reviewing contributions in our annual reviewer acknowledgement.
Stay Current
Review cutting-edge research before publication and stay at the forefront of your academic field.
Global Networking
Connect with researchers and scholars worldwide, building valuable academic relationships across disciplines.
Career Development
Strengthen your academic CV with peer review experience highly valued by universities and research institutions.

Reviewer Requirements

  • Education: PhD, MD, or equivalent terminal degree in a relevant discipline (Master's holders with significant publication record may also apply)
  • Publication Record: Demonstrated research output in peer-reviewed journals in your area(s) of expertise
  • Expertise: Subject matter expertise in one or more of IJMEER's 70+ covered disciplines
  • Time Commitment: Ability to complete reviews within 3–4 weeks of acceptance
  • Ethics: Commitment to confidentiality, objectivity, and constructive feedback
