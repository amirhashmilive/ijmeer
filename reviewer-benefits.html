Reviewer Benefits

IJMEER values your expertise and time. Here's what we offer to our dedicated peer reviewer community.

Official Reviewer Certificate
Receive a signed digital certificate from the IJMEER Editor-in-Chief for each completed review. An excellent credential for academic CVs, promotion applications, and funding proposals.
Editorial Board Invitation
Outstanding reviewers are invited to join IJMEER's Editorial Board as Associate Editors or Board Members. Build your editorial credentials and influence journal direction.
APC Discount
Active reviewers (completed ≥2 reviews per year) receive a discount on Article Processing Charges when they submit their own research to IJMEER. Discounts applied on request.
Exclusive Reviewer Community
Access our private WhatsApp community for IJMEER reviewers — network with fellow academics, discuss research trends, and receive first information about new issues and call-for-papers.
Early Access to Research
Reviewers gain early access to cutting-edge research before publication, keeping you at the forefront of developments in your discipline and related fields.
Public Acknowledgement
All active reviewers are publicly acknowledged in each quarterly issue of IJMEER. Your contribution to academic quality is recognised and credited in our annual reviewer list.

Ready to Contribute?

Join our growing community of peer reviewers and help shape the future of multidisciplinary research. Applications are free and reviewed within 5 business days.
