Who Can Apply?

IJMEER welcomes applications from qualified academics, researchers, and subject-matter experts in any of our 50+ covered disciplines. You do not need to be affiliated with a prestigious institution to become a reviewer — expertise and academic integrity matter most.

✓
Hold a minimum of a Master's degree or equivalent in a relevant field
✓
Have at least one peer-reviewed publication in your area of expertise
✓
Committed to timely, constructive, and unbiased review
✓
PhD scholars, faculty, industry researchers, and independent experts welcome
✓
International reviewers especially encouraged to apply

What Does Reviewing Involve?

1
Invitation
You receive an invitation by email when a manuscript in your area needs review. You may accept or decline — no obligation.
2
Access Manuscript
On accepting, receive the anonymised manuscript via email. Author identity is hidden (double-blind review).
3
Submit Review
Evaluate originality, methodology, presentation, and significance. Submit your structured feedback within 2–3 weeks.
4
Recognition
Receive a Reviewer Certificate. Outstanding reviewers are acknowledged publicly and invited to join the Editorial Board.
Official Certificate
Receive a signed reviewer certificate for every completed review, valuable for academic portfolios and CVs.
Community Access
Join our exclusive reviewer WhatsApp community. Network with researchers from across disciplines worldwide.
APC Discounts
Active reviewers receive discounts on Article Processing Charges when they publish their own research in IJMEER.
