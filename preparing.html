Preparing Your Materials

Follow these guidelines to ensure your manuscript meets IJMEER's formatting and quality requirements before submission.

Author Guidelines

Before submitting your manuscript, ensure it meets IJMEER's requirements. A well-prepared submission speeds up the review process and increases the likelihood of acceptance.

→ View Complete Author Guidelines

Manuscript Format

  • File format: Microsoft Word (.doc or .docx) preferred; PDF for supplementary materials
  • Page size: A4; margins 1 inch (2.54 cm) all sides
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt, double-spaced
  • Length: Research articles 4,000–8,000 words; Review articles up to 10,000 words
  • Line numbers: Include for peer review facilitation

Manuscript Structure

Title Page — Full title, author names, affiliations, corresponding author email, ORCID
Abstract — 200–300 words; structured (Background, Methods, Results, Conclusion)
Keywords — 4–6 keywords for indexing and discoverability
Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion — Standard IMRAD structure recommended
References — APA 7th edition format preferred; numbered Vancouver also accepted

Plagiarism Requirements

All manuscripts are screened for plagiarism. The maximum permissible similarity index is 15% (UGC compliant threshold). Authors may be required to submit a plagiarism report from Turnitin, iThenticate, or an equivalent tool. Self-plagiarism and duplicate submissions are prohibited.

Figures, Tables & Data

  • Figures: minimum 300 DPI resolution; JPEG or PNG format
  • Tables: clear headers, minimal formatting; included in manuscript file
  • All figures/tables must be cited in the main text
  • Captions must be self-explanatory
  • Data tables: use appropriate statistical notation
