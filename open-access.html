Open Access Options

IJMEER is a Gold Open Access journal — all articles are freely available to readers worldwide, immediately upon publication.

Gold Open Access Policy

IJMEER is a fully open-access journal committed to unrestricted access to scholarly research. All published articles are freely available to readers worldwide — no subscriptions, no paywalls, no barriers.

Free to Read Free to Download Free to Share DOI Assigned

Benefits of Open Access

Global Visibility. Research reaches a worldwide audience instantly without access barriers.
Greater Impact. Open access articles are cited more frequently than subscription-gated articles.
Public Good. Publicly funded research becomes freely accessible to taxpayers and society.
Interdisciplinary Discovery. Researchers across disciplines can discover and build upon your work freely.

Creative Commons Licensing

All articles published in IJMEER are licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International License (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0). This means anyone may:

  • Share — copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format
  • Adapt — remix, transform, and build upon the material

Provided they give appropriate credit, do not use for commercial purposes, and distribute adaptations under the same license.
