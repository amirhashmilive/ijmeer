Abstracting & Indexing

IJMEER is indexed in leading academic databases — ensuring maximum global visibility, discoverability, and citation impact for all published research.

Visibility

Why Indexing Matters

Indexing in major academic databases dramatically increases your article's reach, citation potential, and academic influence worldwide.

Discoverability
Articles indexed in major databases appear in academic search engines, institutional repositories, and research portals globally.
Citation Impact
Indexed articles receive significantly more citations. Researchers can only cite what they can find — indexing ensures your work is citable.
DOI Linking
Every IJMEER article receives a permanent Digital Object Identifier — ensuring it remains linkable and citable forever, even if URLs change.
