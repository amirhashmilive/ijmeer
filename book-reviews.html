Book Reviews

IJMEER publishes scholarly book reviews of recent academic publications across all covered disciplines, providing critical evaluation and context for the research community.

About Our Book Reviews

IJMEER's Book Reviews section provides critical assessments of recent academic books published across our 50+ subject areas. Reviews help researchers stay current with important scholarly publications and make informed decisions about reading and citing new works.

Book reviews published in IJMEER are subject to editorial screening (not full peer review) and are published in the same quarterly issues as research articles.

How to Submit a Book Review

Qualified academics, researchers, and graduate students may submit book reviews. The reviewer must have expertise in the book's field and must not have personal/professional connections to the book's author(s).
Books considered for review must: be published within the last 3 years; be academic/scholarly in nature; fall within IJMEER's covered subject areas. Preference given to books that address emerging or cross-disciplinary topics.
  • Length: 800–1,500 words
  • Include full book citation (author, title, publisher, year, ISBN, pages, price)
  • Summarise the book's argument and structure
  • Critically evaluate methodology, originality, and contribution
  • Note any limitations or disagreements
  • Conclude with suitability recommendation for the field
Email your book review to editor@ijmeer.com with subject "Book Review Submission — [Book Title]". Include your full name, affiliation, contact email, and a short bio (50 words). Reviews are accepted on a rolling basis.
