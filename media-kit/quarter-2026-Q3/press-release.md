## IJMEER Announces Call for Papers for Volume 1, Issue 2 (October 2026)

**Raipur, Chhattisgarh, India** — The International Journal of Multidisciplinary Educational Excellence and Research (IJMEER) is pleased to announce its Call for Papers for Volume 1, Issue 2, scheduled for publication in October 2026.

### About IJMEER

IJMEER is a bi-annual, peer-reviewed, Diamond Open Access journal published by Meer Foundation, a registered NGO based in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, India. Established in 2026, IJMEER provides a platform for researchers, academics, and practitioners to publish original research across multidisciplinary fields.

The journal operates under a Diamond Open Access model — meaning all articles are free to read and free to publish, with no Article Processing Charges (APCs). All submissions undergo rigorous double-blind peer review.

### Inaugural Issue Success

IJMEER's inaugural issue (Volume 1, Issue 1) was published in April 2026, featuring peer-reviewed research spanning law, education, management, social sciences, and technology. The response from the academic community has been encouraging.

### Call for Papers — October 2026

IJMEER invites original research articles, review papers, and case studies in the following disciplines:

- **Law & Legal Studies** — Constitutional law, international law, legal education, human rights
- **Education & Pedagogy** — Higher education, curriculum development, educational technology
- **Management & Business** — Strategic management, organizational behaviour, finance
- **Social Sciences & Humanities** — Sociology, psychology, political science, history, philosophy
- **Technology & Innovation** — Computer science, engineering, information technology
- **Environmental Studies** — Sustainability, climate science, environmental policy
- **Public Policy & Governance** — Public administration, policy analysis, governance
