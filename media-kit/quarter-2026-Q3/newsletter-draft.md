## 5. Scholar Spotlight: Ibn Sina (Avicenna)

🌟 This quarter, we honour **Ibn Sina (Avicenna, 980–1037 CE)** — the Persian polymath whose "Canon of Medicine" served as a standard medical reference for over 600 years.

Ibn Sina's multidisciplinary approach — spanning medicine, philosophy, mathematics, and astronomy — embodies the very spirit of IJMEER.
