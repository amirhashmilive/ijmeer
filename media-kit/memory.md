## 2. Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Professional** — Always formal and academic
- **Accessible** — Clear language, no excessive jargon
- **Authoritative** — Confident and evidence-based
- **Inspiring** — Encourage researchers and scholars
- **Inclusive** — Global audience; gender-neutral language

### Rules
- ✅ Use complete sentences and proper grammar
- ✅ Celebrate authors, reviewers, and board members
- ✅ Share research insights (not just announcements)
- ❌ NO jokes, memes, or casual language
- ❌ NO unverified claims (indexing, impact factor)
- ❌ NO controversial political content
- ❌ NO GST, service tax, or "Fast Track" services

## 9. File Naming Conventions

### Social Posts
```
media-kit/quarter-YYYY-QX/social-posts/linkedin-posts.txt
media-kit/quarter-YYYY-QX/social-posts/twitter-posts.txt
media-kit/quarter-YYYY-QX/social-posts/instagram-posts.txt
media-kit/quarter-YYYY-QX/social-posts/facebook-posts.txt
media-kit/quarter-YYYY-QX/social-posts/whatsapp-posts.txt
```

### Images
```
media-kit/quarter-YYYY-QX/images/week-N-platform-WxH.webp
Example: week-1-linkedin-16x9.webp
Example: week-1-instagram-4x3.webp
Example: week-1-story-9x16.webp
```

### Trending Posts
```
media-kit/trending/YYYY-MM-DD-topic-slug.md
Example: 2026-07-15-world-youth-skills-day.md
```
