Open Access Policy

IJMEER is a fully open access journal. All published research is immediately and permanently free for anyone, anywhere to read.

🌐 Open Access

Free to Read. Free to Share. Free Forever.

All IJMEER articles are published under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) license — immediately upon publication, with no embargo period.

Types of Open Access

🥇
Open Access (IJMEER)

Articles published immediately as open access on the publisher's website. No embargo. Author pays APC.

🥈
Green OA (Self-archiving)

Authors may self-archive accepted manuscripts in institutional repositories. IJMEER allows this with no embargo.

Self-Archiving Policy

IJMEER encourages authors to share their work widely. Authors may self-archive:

  • Preprints: Any version before peer review — on preprint servers (arXiv, SSRN, etc.) at any time
  • Accepted manuscript: The peer-reviewed, accepted version — in institutional repositories or personal websites immediately after acceptance, with a link back to the published article
  • Published PDF: The final published PDF may be shared freely under CC BY 4.0 with full attribution
