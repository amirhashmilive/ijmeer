Payment Instructions

1
Submit Manuscript
First submit your manuscript via our Google Form. Do NOT pay before receiving an acceptance decision.
2
Receive Decision
After peer review, you will receive an acceptance letter via email (typically 4–6 weeks after submission).
3
Pay APC
Upon acceptance, pay the APC via Razorpay (credit/debit card, UPI, net banking) or contact us for bank transfer details.
4
Publication
After payment confirmation, your article is scheduled for publication.
