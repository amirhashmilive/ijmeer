About This Journal

IJMEER is an Open Access multidisciplinary research journal published by Meer Foundation, dedicated to advancing scholarly knowledge across disciplines.

Aims & Scope

The International Journal of Multidisciplinary Explication and Emerging Research (IJMEER) is a peer-reviewed, Open Access journal committed to publishing original, high-quality research across a broad spectrum of academic disciplines. IJMEER provides a platform for scholars, researchers, and practitioners to share their findings with the global academic community.

The journal welcomes theoretical, empirical, applied, and review articles that contribute meaningfully to the advancement of knowledge. IJMEER embraces interdisciplinary and multidisciplinary perspectives, recognising that the most innovative research often emerges at the intersection of traditional fields.

We particularly encourage submissions that address emerging trends, novel methodologies, contemporary challenges, and cross-disciplinary solutions relevant to today’s dynamic research landscape.

Review Policy

✓
Reviews must be of academic books relevant to IJMEER’s multidisciplinary scope
✓
Length: 800–1,500 words (structured critical analysis, not merely descriptive)
✓
Must include full bibliographic details of the reviewed book
✓
Reviewer must disclose any conflict of interest with the author or publisher
✓
Reviews undergo editorial assessment before acceptance
✓
Published open access alongside regular research articles

What to Include

1. Reviewer Information
Full name, affiliation, email address, and brief bio
2. Book Details
Title, author(s), publisher, year, ISBN, and edition
3. Critical Analysis
Summary, evaluation of arguments, contribution to field, strengths and limitations
4. Recommendation
Target audience and overall recommendation for the scholarly community
