## 2. Brand Voice

### Principles

| Principle | Description |
|---|---|
| **Professional** | Always formal and academic in tone |
| **Accessible** | Clear language — avoid jargon where possible |
| **Authoritative** | Confident, evidence-based |
| **Inspiring** | Encourage researchers and scholars |
| **Inclusive** | Global audience; gender-neutral language |

### DO:
- Use complete sentences and proper grammar
- Celebrate authors, reviewers, and board members
- Share research insights (not just announcements)
- Engage with comments professionally
- Tag affiliated institutions when appropriate

### DON'T:
- Post jokes, memes, or casual language
- Make unverified claims (indexing, impact factor, etc.)
- Post controversial political content
- Share others' full papers without attribution
- Ignore comments or messages for more than 48 hours

## 7. Engagement Guidelines

- Respond to all comments within **48 hours**
- Thank authors who share their published articles
- Repost affiliated institution posts when tagging IJMEER
- Do NOT engage with spam, trolling, or abusive comments
- Block/report abusive accounts
- Do not endorse political candidates or parties

---

## 8. Crisis Communication

If incorrect information is shared or a post causes controversy:
1. Do NOT delete the post without consulting the Managing Editor
2. Draft a correction/clarification statement
3. Get approval from Dr. Nusrat Ali Hashmi or Sayed Amir Mustafa Hashmi
4. Post correction with: "Correction: [statement]"
5. Pin the correction if significant
