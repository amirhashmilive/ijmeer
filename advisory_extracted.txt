 01 Prof. (Dr.) Mona Purohit Required Details Field Information Full Name Prof. (Dr.) Mona Purohit Designation Dean &amp; Head, Department of Legal Studies and Research Institutional Affiliation Barkatullah Vishwavidyalaya, Hoshangabad Road, Bhopal -- 462026, Madhya Pradesh, India Official Profile Link https://bubhopal.ac.in/Site/Upload/Menu/1119_1133_Legal-Studies-And-Research_co.pdf Detailed Profile Prof. (Dr.) Mona Purohit is a distinguished legal academician, researcher, and author who serves as the Dean and Head of the Department of Legal Studies and Research at Barkatullah Vishwavidyalaya, Bhopal. With over three decades of experience in legal education, she has established herself as a transformative figure in curriculum development, legal research methodology, intellectual property law, environmental law, cyber law, and judicial process. She is recognized as the youngest person to attain an LL.D. (post-doctorate) in Central India. Academic Credentials Dr. Purohit holds a Doctor of Laws (LL.D.), a post-doctorate degree awarded in 2020 for her thesis titled "Patenting of Biotechnological Products in India: Challenges in Harmonisation of the Conflicting Interests." She earned her Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Law in 2004 for her thesis "Critical Study of Wildlife Laws and Its Impact on Tribes." She holds an LL.M. in Modern Legal Studies (1998), an LL.B. (1996), and a B.Sc . (1993). She also holds a Certificate in Computer Education from the State Council of Education Research &amp; Training, Madhya Pradesh (1989). Leadership at Barkatullah University As Dean and Head of the Department of Legal Studies and Research, Dr. Purohit leads a department established in 1982 offering B.A. LL.B. (Hons.), LL.M., Ph.D., and LL.D. degrees. Under her leadership, the department has embraced modern teaching techniques, mandatory weekly presentations, transparent monthly tests, legal literacy camps, moot court competitions, and placement assistance. Her vision emphasizes global deployment of legal talent rather than merely national conventional employment. The department produces legal scholars capable of meeting challenges of global corporations and cross-border population movement. Dr. Purohit has served as Head of Department (April 2010-December 2013; April-December 2014; since June 2019), Dean of the Faculty of Law (since June 2019), and Member of the Executive Council of Barkatullah University (since August 2020). She has also served as a Consultant for a two-year project of the National Law Institute University and the United Nations Fund for Population Activities (UNFPA) on the PCPNDT Act. Major Publications: Books Dr. Purohit has authored four significant books serving as essential textbooks for LL.M. students across India. "Legal Education and Research Methodology" (4th edition, 2024) is a key textbook designed for the LL.M. curriculum as prescribed by the UGC. The book is divided into two parts: the first focuses on methods of legal teaching including lectures, seminars, and clinical methods; the second covers the entire process of legal research including hypothesis formulation, data collection, research design, and report writing. "Judicial Process" (2024 edition) addresses another compulsory subject for LL.M. students. The book covers various theories of attaining justice, judicial discretion, judicial activism, and the relationship between law and justice. A separate chapter on emerging trends elaborates on landmark case laws that have shaped Indian jurisprudence. "Wild Life Laws and its Impact on Tribes" (2007) examines the intersection of wildlife conservation laws and their legal and social impact on tribal communities in India. The work critically analyzes the Wildlife Protection Act, forest rights legislation, and constitutional safeguards for tribal populations. Dr. Purohit has also published over 30 research papers in national and international journals. Research Supervision Dr. Purohit has supervised 14 candidates to the award of Ph.D. degrees, with five additional scholars currently pursuing Ph.D. under her guidance. She also supervised an ICSSR post-doctoral fellowship on "Legal Study of Sexual Harassment at Workplace." Conference Presentations Dr. Purohit has presented papers at numerous international conferences, including the XIX ISA World Congress of Sociology in Toronto, Canada (2018), the 5th International Congress of Environmental Research in Malaysia (2012), and the 2nd International Conference in Goa (2015). She was an invited speaker at the University of Malaysia Terengganu and has served as a resource person for workshops organized by the UGC-Human Resource Development Centre, RCVP NORONHA Academy of Administration, and SSB Academy. Awards and Honors Dr. Purohit has received the "Lifetime Golden Achievement Award" from the Social &amp; Legal Research Foundation, Varanasi (2020), the "Vidhi Gaurav Award" (2021), the "Best Law Teacher 2020-21" award from the University of Allahabad, the "Shiksha Ratan Puraskar" from the India International Friendship Society (2011), and multiple "Best Teacher" awards from Barkatullah University and Lions Club, Bhopal. Editorial Roles Dr. Purohit serves as Patron of the International Research and Review, Associate Editor of Cyber Affair, Associate Editor of the Indian Human Rights Law Review, and Member of the Editorial Board of Journal Adhikaar, Law &amp; Society, Research Diary, and Fifteen Days. Expertise Dr. Purohit's expertise spans Intellectual Property Laws, Environmental Laws, Cyber Law, Public Laws, Jurisprudence, Research Methodology, Interpretation of Statutes, Judicial Process, and Gender Justice. She has actively participated in shaping the curriculum of various universities across India. Legacy Prof. (Dr.) Mona Purohit represents a model of integrated legal academic leadership, where doctoral research informs institutional vision, publications shape legal discourse, and administrative leadership creates opportunities for students.
