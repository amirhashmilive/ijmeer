Process

Step-by-Step Submission Guide

1
Prepare Your Manuscript
Ensure your manuscript follows our author guidelines: correct formatting (Times New Roman 12pt, double-spaced, APA 7th references), structured abstract, 5–8 keywords, and author details on a separate title page. Run plagiarism check — must be below 20%.
2
Check the Submission Window
IJMEER accepts submissions year-round, but publication follows quarterly deadlines. Check the quarterly schedule to know the next publication opportunity. Late submissions after the deadline incur a Fast Track surcharge or are held for the next issue.
3
Complete the Submission Form
Click "Open Submission Form" above to access our Google Form. You'll be asked to provide: paper title, author details, abstract, keywords, subject area, and to upload your manuscript file (Word .docx preferred). Have all materials ready before starting.
4
Receive Confirmation
Within 3 business days, you will receive an acknowledgement email at your provided address confirming your submission has been received and is under initial editorial screening.
5
Peer Review & Decision
Your manuscript enters double-blind peer review. You'll receive a decision within 4–6 weeks (or 2–3 weeks for Fast Track). Decisions include: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, or Reject; each with detailed reviewer feedback.
6
Pay APC & Publication
On acceptance, pay the Article Processing Charge via Razorpay (?2000/USD 75). After payment confirmation, your article is typeset, assigned a DOI, and published in the next quarterly issue. Full open-access immediately upon publication.
