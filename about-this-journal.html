About IJMEER

INTERNATIONAL JOURNAL OF MULTIDISCIPLINARY EXPLICATION AND EMERGING RESEARCH — a premier open-access platform advancing scholarship across every academic discipline, published by Meer Foundation.

Our Mission

To provide a prestigious, peer-reviewed, open-access platform for researchers across all academic disciplines to publish high-quality scholarship that advances global knowledge, bridges disciplinary boundaries, and addresses humanity's most pressing emerging challenges. IJMEER is committed to making scholarly research freely accessible to readers worldwide at no cost, while supporting authors through a transparent and efficient publication process.

Our Vision

To be recognized internationally as the leading multidisciplinary journal championing open science, equitable access to knowledge, and collaborative research that transcends traditional academic silos — connecting scholars from emerging and developed nations alike. We envision a world where high-quality research, regardless of discipline or geography, reaches its deserved global audience.

Aims & Scope

Aims & Scope of IJMEER

The International Journal of Multidisciplinary Explication and Emerging Research (IJMEER) is a peer-reviewed, open-access scholarly journal published quarterly by Meer Foundation, Dhamtari, Chhattisgarh, India. IJMEER is dedicated to facilitating scholarly discourse across a broad spectrum of academic disciplines. The journal serves as a forum for researchers, academicians, practitioners, and students to disseminate original research, theoretical innovations, and practical applications.

Original Empirical Research
IJMEER publishes novel empirical studies presenting new data, observations, or experimental findings that contribute meaningfully to the existing body of knowledge across any academic discipline.
Comprehensive Review Articles
Systematic and narrative reviews that synthesize existing research on emerging topics, identify knowledge gaps, and chart directions for future inquiry across disciplines.
Case Studies & Research Notes
In-depth analyses of specific real-world phenomena, policy implementations, legal cases, educational interventions, or organizational transformations with clear theoretical and practical implications.
Interdisciplinary & Cross-Disciplinary Research
Studies that bridge multiple academic fields, drawing on methodologies and theoretical frameworks from different disciplines to address complex real-world problems that cannot be addressed within a single disciplinary framework.
Emerging & Frontier Research
Pioneering investigations into newly emerging fields, technologies, social phenomena, and disciplinary frontiers — including AI ethics, climate policy, digital health, legal innovation, sustainable development, and more.
Policy Analysis & Social Commentaries
Evidence-based policy analyses, critical commentaries, and scholarly perspectives on significant social, political, economic, legal, and environmental developments with implications for public discourse and governance.

Editorial Policies

IJMEER Editorial Policies

Peer Review Policy

All submissions to IJMEER undergo rigorous double-blind peer review, where neither authors nor reviewers know each other's identities. Each manuscript is evaluated by at least two independent expert reviewers from the relevant field. The review process typically takes 4—6 weeks. Manuscripts are assessed on originality, scholarly rigor, methodological soundness, clarity of writing, and contribution to the field.

Plagiarism & Originality Policy

IJMEER is committed to publishing only original research. All submissions are screened for plagiarism. Manuscripts must have a similarity index of 15% or below (UGC compliant threshold) to be considered for peer review. Self-plagiarism, duplicate submissions, and recycled content are strictly prohibited. Authors may be required to submit a plagiarism report generated by an approved tool (Turnitin, iThenticate, or equivalent).

Open Access Policy

IJMEER is a Gold Open Access journal. All articles are immediately and permanently free to read, download, and share upon publication. Authors retain copyright under a Creative Commons license. All publication costs are covered through Article Processing Charges (APC) paid by authors or their institutions, ensuring no financial barrier for readers.

Publication Ethics

IJMEER adheres to the ethical guidelines established by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE). We are committed to preventing research fraud, ensuring proper authorship attribution, maintaining fair and transparent peer review, and handling conflicts of interest, corrections, and retractions professionally. All parties — authors, reviewers, and editors — are expected to uphold the highest standards of research integrity.

Article Processing Charges (APC)

IJMEER operates on an author-pays model to sustain Gold Open Access. The APC is & #8377;2,000 for Indian authors and USD 75 for international authors. Fast-track publication is available at additional cost. APC waivers may be granted in exceptional cases — contact the editorial office for details. No submission fee is charged; the APC is only payable upon acceptance.

Gold Open Access Commitment

IJMEER is a Gold Open Access journal. All articles are immediately and permanently free for everyone to read, download, copy, and distribute under an open license. We believe that knowledge should have no barriers — economic, geographic, or institutional.
