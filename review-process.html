Peer Review Process

IJMEER operates a rigorous double-blind peer review process — ensuring fairness, objectivity, and the highest standards of academic integrity for every submitted manuscript.

Principles

Our Review Principles

Double-Blind
Both authors and reviewers remain anonymous to each other, eliminating bias and ensuring objective evaluation purely on scientific merit.
Timely
First decisions within 4–6 weeks. We respect authors' time and maintain transparent communication at every stage of the process.
Transparent
Authors receive detailed reviewer feedback with clear guidance for revision or reasons for rejection. No black-box decisions.
Step by Step

Review Timeline

1
Days 1–3
Submission & Initial Screening
Author submits via Google Form. Editorial team performs initial quality checks: scope fit, formatting, anti-plagiarism screening (max 15% similarity, UGC compliant). Acknowledgement email sent within 3 business days.
2
Days 3–7
Editor Assignment
Managing Editor assigns to a Subject Area Editor with relevant expertise. Editor assesses suitability and assigns 2–3 qualified peer reviewers from IJMEER's expert panel.
3
Weeks 2–5
Peer Review Evaluation
Reviewers evaluate the manuscript on: originality, methodology, results validity, discussion quality, references, and writing. Each reviewer submits a structured report with a numerical score and recommendation.
4
Week 5–6
Editorial Decision
Subject Editor consolidates reviews and makes one of four decisions: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, or Reject. Decision communicated to authors with complete reviewer feedback.
5
Week 6–8
Revision & Final Acceptance
Authors address reviewer comments and resubmit. Revised manuscript reviewed by editor. Upon final acceptance, production begins — galley proof, DOI assignment, and publication scheduling.
Outcomes

Possible Editorial Decisions

✓ Accept
Manuscript accepted as-is or with only minor typographical corrections. Ready for production.
⟳ Minor Revision
Small changes required. Author responds within 2 weeks; re-review typically not needed.
⚠ Major Revision
Significant rework required. Author responds within 4 weeks; second peer review may occur.
✕ Reject
Manuscript does not meet IJMEER's standards. No APC is charged. Detailed reasons provided.
