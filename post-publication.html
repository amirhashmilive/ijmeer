Post-Publication Impact

Maximize the reach and real-world impact of your IJMEER published research.

Amplify Your Research Impact

Publishing with IJMEER is just the beginning. Open access publication gives your research a global audience — but to maximize citation impact and visibility, proactive post-publication dissemination is key.

Global Sharing
Share your article link on ResearchGate, Academia.edu, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X to reach a worldwide audience.
Academic Profiles
Add your article to Google Scholar, ORCID, Scopus Author Profiles, Publons, and Semantic Scholar.
Repositories
Deposit in institutional repositories, PubMed Central, arXiv, SSRN, or subject-specific databases.

Strategies to Increase Citation Impact

1
Write a plain-language summary — Create a blog post or summary accessible to non-specialists. Share on institutional blogs, LinkedIn, or ResearchGate.
2
Present at conferences — Reference your published IJMEER article in conference presentations to drive awareness and citations.
3
Use your article link — Always cite your article using its DOI link in emails, signatures, CV, and social media to ensure discoverability.
4
Email key researchers — Directly share your article with colleagues and researchers in your field who may find it relevant.
5
Update your CV & profiles — Add the article to your academic CV, institutional profile, Google Scholar, ORCID, and ResearchGate profile immediately.
