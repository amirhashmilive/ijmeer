Submitting Your Materials

Step-by-step guide to submitting your manuscript to IJMEER for peer review and publication.

How to Submit

IJMEER accepts submissions via our online Google Form. Please prepare all required documents before starting your submission. For the most complete guide, see our full submission guide.

Submission Steps

1
Prepare Your Manuscript
Format your manuscript per our author guidelines. Ensure similarity index =15%. Use Word format (.docx).
2
Write Your Cover Letter
State why your manuscript is suitable for IJMEER, confirm it's original and not under review elsewhere, and declare any conflicts of interest.
3
Submit via Google Form
Click the Submit button below. Fill in all required fields: author details, abstract, keywords, and upload your manuscript and any supplementary files.
4
Receive Acknowledgement
You will receive an acknowledgement email within 2–3 working days confirming receipt of your submission and providing a manuscript reference number.
5
Peer Review (4–6 Weeks)
Your manuscript will undergo double-blind peer review. You will receive a decision (Accept / Minor Revision / Major Revision / Reject) within 4–6 weeks.
