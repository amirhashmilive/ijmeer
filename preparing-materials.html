Preparing Your Manuscript

Comprehensive author guidelines to help you prepare a submission that meets IJMEER's formatting, ethical, and scholarly standards.

Before you begin: Ensure your manuscript is original, unpublished, and not under review by any other journal. IJMEER uses plagiarism detection tools — manuscripts with >15% similarity index are desk-rejected (UGC compliant threshold).
Scope

Article Types We Accept

Original Research
New empirical findings, experimental data, or theoretical contributions. Typically 4,000–8,000 words.
Review Articles
Comprehensive synthesis of existing literature on an emerging topic. Typically 6,000–12,000 words.
Case Studies
In-depth investigation of a specific real-world case. Typically 3,000–6,000 words with clear learning outcomes.

Ethics

Ethical Requirements

Authorship Criteria
All listed authors must have made substantial intellectual contributions. Ghost authorship and guest authorship are not permitted.
Conflict of Interest
Authors must disclose all financial, professional, or personal relationships that could influence their work.
Data Availability
Authors are encouraged to share datasets used in their research via a public repository and include a data availability statement.
Human/Animal Studies
Research involving human participants or animals must have appropriate ethics committee approval. Certificate copy required.
